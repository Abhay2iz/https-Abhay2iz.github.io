Barriers to Success

By Abhay Agrahari, written at age 18

Introduction

We human beings are always in pursuit of success, or we try to become successful. Success is defined differently for different people, but the fundamentals of success are the same. This article will focus on the essential principles required to achieve success.

Fundamentals of Success

At various stages of life, we set goals and try different approaches to achieve them. A businessperson may aim to increase profits, an aspirant may work toward passing an exam, a social media influencer may strive to post content regularly, and a sportsperson may dream of representing their country.

After setting a goal, we begin working toward it. The efforts and process we follow will ultimately determine our fate.

1. Making a Plan and Strategy

This is the first and most crucial step on the journey to success. Creating a clear and effective plan is vital. At this stage, you should seek guidance from someone experienced in your field. Having a mentor or guide to support you can be extremely beneficial.

2. Discipline

Discipline is perhaps the most important factor. To stick with your plan and implement it consistently, you need to put in work every day. That sustained effort, even when you don’t feel motivated, is what defines discipline.

Barriers to Success

1. Negative Characteristics

These internal habits or mindsets hold us back, including:

  • Procrastination
  • Distractions
  • Fear of failure
  • Self-doubt
  • Jealousy

2. Uncontrollable Circumstances

Despite doing everything right, unexpected situations may arise, such as:

  • Becoming seriously ill just before an important exam
  • Losing a family member during a crucial period

These are events outside your control.

Tackling the Barriers

Overcoming Negative Characteristics

Recognize the importance of your goal. Remind yourself why you started and visualize the finish. For long-term goals, create a monthly and daily plan. Execution with consistency matters most.

Never give up unless it’s truly over. If you quit, you gain nothing. But if you stay in the game, you will earn something—skills, growth, or success.

Facing Uncontrollable Circumstances

You can’t always stop these events, but you can choose how to respond. Start by detaching from the outcome and placing trust in faith.

Spirituality can bring peace and strength during tough times. Belief in a higher power helps you face both internal and external challenges with calm and clarity.

Conclusion

Success requires more than smart planning and hard work. Overcoming both internal and external barriers is just as important. Stay disciplined, stay focused, and stay committed to your purpose. Let faith carry you through what you cannot control.
